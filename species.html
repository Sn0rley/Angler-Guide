Species Overview

Common species in many freshwater systems and quick notes on where to find them.

  • Largemouth Bass — Preferred habitats: weedy shorelines, submerged structure, and docks. Try topwater lures early morning.
  • Channel Catfish — Active at dusk and night; bait anglers often succeed with cut bait or stink baits near deeper holes.
  • Rainbow Trout — Cold-water streams and stocked lakes; use light tackle, small spinners, and natural baits.
  • Bluegill — Shallow weedy bays; great for beginners and kids using small hooks and live bait.

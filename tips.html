Fishing Tips

  • Check local weather and water conditions before heading out.
  • Match your bait or lure to the species and water clarity.
  • Practice catch and release when populations are stressed; handle fish with wet hands and minimize air exposure.
  • Keep a log of what works: location, time, bait, and conditions help you learn faster.

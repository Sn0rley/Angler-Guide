Gear Checklist

  • Rod and reel appropriate for target species (light, medium, heavy).

    • An assortment of different rods and reels

  • Assortment of hooks, weights, lures, and spare line.

    • A simple freshwater tackle box

  • Life jacket, first-aid kit, sun protection, and drinking water.

    • In case of any emergencies and accidents

  • Net, pliers, measuring tape, and a small cooler or livewell if keeping fish.

    • Pliers help get hooks out of the fish's mouth

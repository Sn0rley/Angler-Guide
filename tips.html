Fishing Tips

  • Check local weather and water conditions before heading out.
    • Recommended App: BassForecast

    A helpful tool for predicting bass activity, weather patterns, and ideal fishing windows.

  • Match your bait or lure to the species and water clarity.

    • Quick guide: best lure colors for murky vs. clear water.

  • Practice catch and release when populations are stressed; handle fish with wet hands and minimize air exposure.

    • Example of a safe way to release rainbow trout.

  • Keep a log of what works: location, time, bait, and conditions help you learn faster.

    • Keep a journal like this and write down things that work or don't work to be consistent.
